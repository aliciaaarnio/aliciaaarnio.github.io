Solar-Stellar Connections

February 15, 2017

CU Boulder, Space Sciences Building (SPSC), room W120

Workshop Announcement, 1/12/17: Solar-Stellar Connections will be a half-day workshop aiming to bring together solar and stellar researchers in the Boulder area to meet, share research, and forge collaborations. Motivated by the near-complete transition of NSO headquarters to Boulder, Solar-Stellar Connections will feature introductory talks, posters, and discussion time. Our scientific focus will be the shared interest of studying stars and their astrospheres, our own and those afar.


The meeting format will be like a large group meeting: we encourage attendees to give talks introducing themselves, their work, and even putting out calls for collaborative help on specific projects. We also encourage bringing posters from recent meetings as there will be ample discussion breaks for meeting and talking.


To give the organizers an idea of attendance, please RSVP via this google form and indicate if you would like to give a talk and/or poster; our time allocations for talks will be determined by attendance. This google form is editable after the fact; titles are not required to register.


Organizers: Alicia Aarnio (LASP/NSO) and Adam Kowalski (CU/NSO).
